# Action

Actions are NFTs that hold within them the record of work.

littlefish perform real-world activities.&#x20;

* A group of friends cleaning litter on the beach.&#x20;
* A digital community collaborating towards social/environmental impact.
* A DAO creating blockchain solutions for a use case.
* A business announcing/developing/producing a new product.&#x20;
* A charity organizing a new campaign to build houses for those in need.&#x20;
